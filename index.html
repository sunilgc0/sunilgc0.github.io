HotSpot Analysis of New York Taxis

ACM Sigspatial Cup 2016

The top 50 hotspot locations for New York city yellow taxi dataset based on their z-scores have to be found. Only the pickup locations are considered for the hotspot analysis. The New York city taxi dataset for Jan 2015 is considered for the analysis. A space time cube is created that contains spatial neighborhood for preceding, current and following time periods with each cell having 26 neighbors. The space and time needs to be aggregated into cube cells that forms a space-time cube.
